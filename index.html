Muffin & Waffle

Muffin & Waffle LORE

Muffin a Siamese cat whose daily routine is napping, clinging to humans like Velcro, and looking offended when asked to move. Muffin operates on “low battery mode” 24/7, recharging only through unlimited cuddles. Truly the definition of a furry power bank—minus the power.

Waffle a Puspin with the energy of three espressos and the attitude of a seasoned action star. One minute he’s demanding cuddles, the next he’s starting imaginary boxing matches with the air. Known for his suplado stares that say, “Don’t touch me... unless I say so.” Basically, he’s the neighborhood tough guy—if the neighborhood is your living room.

Together, they’re chaos and calm, clinginess and aloofness, nap time and fight night. While Muffin is draped lazily across the couch, Waffle is probably mid-air, attempting a WWE elbow drop on thin air—or on Muffin, who will ignore it because he’s too tired for this nonsense.
